Also, my neighborhood's biannual event, Cherrywood Plant & Book Swap is this
Saturday 10a to Noon at Cherrywood Rd and E 34th St. 

 

This is a uke-friendly event. In the past, we have had performances from
Bill and Kathy Jones, Maplewood Elementary School Ukulele Choir (please ck
out their Youtube video below) and others. 

 

It's free and you don't have to bring anything if you don't want to. See
links below for details. 

 

So if you attend consider bringing your ukulele (please leave your cowbells
at home tho :)

 

Please forward this event info to interested parties. 

 

Web <http://cherrywoodswap.com/PlantSwap.php> 

Facebook event <https://www.facebook.com/events/989879684404497/>
