One of the folks I work with is on the HAMM board, and a few months back
Folk Uke played a HAMM benefit at the new Google space on W. 2nd Street
(part of the old Austin Children's Museum site).  We went to the benefit
and got a chance to meet and visit with Amy and Cathy before they played,
you couldn't ever expect to meet nicer folks.  I had seen them play on
WoodSongs (great program on PBS, by the way, for anyone not familiar with
it) a few months prior and so they both laughed when I told them about
seeing them on the show.  I couldn't make it last night, but I hope there
was a good turnout for them.
