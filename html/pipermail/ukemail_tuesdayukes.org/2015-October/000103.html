Uh  - no - it’s always a tilde - whether it’s used as a suspension mark (like your example) or as a diacritical mark (as a character itself). 

A tilde is a tilde is a tilde. 

In the case of California Stars it’s used correctly to indicate something missing (or as we use it, a pause).
