I hope this is the final version for Time Warp. Please note that I had the lead break in the wrong place; it comes between the final two choruses. Also, during the lead break we should do short single strums. The chords for the lead break look like this: 

A  /  Bb7~B7  /  G  /  D  /  A  /  A  /  Bb7~B7
It's a single strum with a hard stop for the A chord, followed by a slide up from a Bb7 to the B7 (sounding like two strums) before the hard stop. 

You can practice it with the video at https://search.yahoo.com/yhs/search?p=time+warp+rocky+horror+picture+show&ei=UTF-8&hspart=mozilla&hsimp=yhs-001. The lead break (tap dance part) comes at about the 3:22 mark. 

I hope this helps us get it right. Thanks for your patience.
