I have changed strings a lot, usually until I find a set I like and even
then I will probably change them after awhile.  But it is really up to the
individual, I know some folks who still have the original strings on their
ukes.  I think it also has to due with the uke and how it is made, and the
feel a person prefers.  I would be careful about the gauge of the 3rd(C)
string, in some sets these are to thick. If you are going to set up with
Low G I would recommend the Fremont Soloist Wound Squekless Polished
String, I haven't tried the other Fremont strings.
