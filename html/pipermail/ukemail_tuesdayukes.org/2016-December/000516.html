Hey,

 

Tonight I will bring copies of a piece that I am working on (I call it The
Piedmont Rag which I'm told is a Canadian Christmas song). Pls see
attachment for my slap dash chord chart. 

 

I also attached a sample strum track (at 80 bpm). Ideally, this would be
played a bit faster but this is a good tempo for learning the changes.

 

When it repeats, you may want to try triplets at measures 9 and 10. 

 

Then at measure 12 when you get to the diminished seventh chord, if you
wanna get fancy, you can cascade up the fretboard playing on beat 1= 2323
then beat 2=5656 then 3=8989 then 4=11|12|11|12 (but this happens quickly). 

 

Jack Darby
