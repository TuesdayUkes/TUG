No TUG this week - next TUG is an iTUG - on 3rd Jan.

I've made some changes to Make Your Chair Hang Low - I added some 
ornament to the last phrase in the Uke 1 part, and added the intro and 
tag at the end to all the parts (I might have missed some TABs - so 
check the part you're using). I also put together a simple Uke 2 harmony 
part - if we get enough people playing it might be nice to add that in - 
and we could sing it as well - especially on the last Chair Hang Low. I 
cleaned up some of the mistakes - but no doubt there are plenty left.

For chords/singing - the Uke1-Chords PDF is probably the best bet.

The new files are on the Dropbox site - and should all have the new date 
in them.

https://www.dropbox.com/sh/cwy0f10rjd1s8bw/AACTqwbWRBi636Vdiy7-Gz_Ea?dl=0

The South Austin Splinter TUG has expressed an interest in playing with 
us on Chair Hang Low - so they'll probably show up to one of the TUG 
sessions before the AUS meeting on Jan 12.
