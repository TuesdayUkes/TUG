> If you've never seen the Purple Martins roosting here in Austin - this is a great year for it. 
> 
> This year they're roosting at the EMbassy Suites at 35 &290  - there is a great little lawn to sit on - and for some reason they've swooping right down on top of the lawn as they come in - it's like being in a river of birds. They start coming in right at dusk ~8:40 - and all of them are roosted by the time the sun has fully set - about 30 minutes. There are about 500,000 birds in the roost. It's amazing. Travis Audubon has an information tent set up on Fridays and Saturdays - but the birds are there every night. 
> 
> http://travisaudubon.org/purple-martin-parties
> 
> Embassy Suites asks that people coming to see the birds please park on the eastern side of their parking lot.
