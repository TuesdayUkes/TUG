Hey, 

 

Tomorrow is our neighborhood garden event (details below) but it is also a
ukulele playing opportunity and the weather should be nice. I can count at
least 5 people from our ukulele community that have attended before and a
few have played music (uke music, of course) there. And this time we will
not have the nearby elementary school ukulele choir performing so it is even
more wide open.

If you can drop by shoot for 11a-noon when the event should be hopping.

Texas 78722).

paraphernalia (recorded media, sheet music and musical instruments). Take
