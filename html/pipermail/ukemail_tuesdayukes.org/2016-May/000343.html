We had a blast playing this last week in TUG! The original is in Db, and
it's in C in the Halifax Ukulele Gang songbook
<https://halifaxukulelegang.wordpress.com/hug-songbook/>, but Betsy thinks
G would be a good key.

Give it a listen and see if there's a part you'd like to play or sing!

Here's the Jimmy Fallon/Melissa McCarthy lip-synch battle Deanna shared
that started it all.
