Hello fans and friends!
After a very busy fall traveling making music and teaching workshops, we are happy to be back in our new house and engaging in some extreme nesting.  We are hard at work renovating our new woodworking shop, catching up on Nicole's craft orders, fixing up the house and keeping up with the Mya-Moe shop schedule.  We have been playing locally, both as The Quiet American and Aaron with The Junebug Boys.

We don't have too much to report right now when it comes to The Quiet American, but please know we are looking forward to 2017 with new books, audio and video for you as well as some great festivals and music camps.

For your viewing pleasure, below are two videos, one from our Folk Song Garden<http://quietamericanmusic.us3.list-manage1.com/track/click?u=b737a681eb659085ad4266c8f&id=64ac148795&e=82d7f313c8> book and one by The Junebug Boys.  Enjoy!
