So this Tuesday we're meeting at Central Market at 7:00 - probably 
downstairs or upstairs or inside or outside - we'll see when we get 
there were we can get a spot that's not too noisy. Come out and have a

Recreation Center in the downstairs classroom. Hancock is at 811 E. 41st

also the entrance for Hancock Golf Course. We haven't seen the room yet

Rec will post TUG up on their web site, and we'll be added to their 
catalog of activities. We'll add the address for the meeting to our post 
on the Austin Ukulele Society web site as well - so it should make it 
simpler for those folks to find us. Austin Rec has asked us to provide 
some TUG pictures that they can use on their web site - so we'll be 
doing snaps over the next few meetings.

See you all at Central Market
