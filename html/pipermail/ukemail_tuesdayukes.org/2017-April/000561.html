Apologies to anyone I already sent this to - but here's a strumming 
practice sheet.

Use a metronome, and start at a speed where you can do each measure easily.
Keep your hand relaxed, and keep your motion steady. Keep your hand 
moving when there is a rest, just don't hit the strings.
It can help to count  - 1+2+3+4+ - as you strum - don't say the count 
where there is a rest. eg - 1 2+ 3+ 4+

A good way to practice strumming is to strum on your leg when you're 
walking - keep in time to your pace.

I do the exercise on the sheet everyday, repeating each measure 4 times. 
I pick a random speed on my metronome. This is a core skill provides the 
foundation for all kinds of complex strumming.
