I'm just wondering if there is any interest out there in TUG land in a 
weekly Practice Group.

We would meet on Mondays 7-7:30ish for an hour or so, probably at the 
Hancock Recreation Center.

The idea would be to pick something to work on for a month at a time. 
Ideas might include:

1) Arpeggio studies (Daniel Ward, Samantha Muir)
2) Campanella - Samantha Muir, Rob McKillop
3) Triplet strums
4) Movable chords
5) Right hand techniques
6) Irish tunes
7) Fingerpicking

Someone suggests something, we split it into 4 weekly blocks,  and make 
a goal to work on each block for a week. On Mondays we play through what 
we've worked on. The notion is to just give ourselves a weekly goal.

My feeling is it would be good (or at least interesting) to mix up focus 
each month - we could always decide as a group to extend or repeat 
something.

If you're interested in something like this holler at me - and I'll talk 
to Austin Parks and Rec about getting room.
