Our next iTUG is next Tuesday - March 14 - 7:00 at the Hancock 
Recreation Center.

I have a project for the Ukulele Institute teacher training that I need 
some help with. I have a canon (round) I put together based on an 8-bar 
blues called Trouble in Mind. It's pretty simple. I'd like to see if we 
could work it up and play it together. I need to get a recording of it 
for my class.

Attached is a practice sheet (all the parts are the same), along with an 
Mp3 of practice sheet - and one with the complete piece (all the parts 
together).

We'll see how that goes.

Here's what we'll go over:

1) Happy Trails - all the way through, Singing,chords, Uke 1, Uke 2
2) Stray Cat Strut - Section B - play/repeat until nauseous. Make a pass 
at Section C. Play A,B, and as much of C as we can stand.
3) Yakka Hickey Dula - Intro, Section A, Section B. Make a pass at Section C
4) Trouble in Mind - Play together, play the U1,2,3 parts together

I'll get fresh print outs of most of the pieces and make sure I have 
them with me.
