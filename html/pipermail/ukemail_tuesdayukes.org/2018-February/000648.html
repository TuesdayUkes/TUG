Here's the B section of Lilting Banshee. We'll go through this next week like we've gone through the A section. 

For the folks who want to play chords - start by aplying just 2 strums per measure - on the 1 and 4 beats. This will let you get the changes under your fingers. If you want to practice the arpeggio - just use open strings and pluck

1-2-3-1-4-3 - one finger per string. Go slowly.
