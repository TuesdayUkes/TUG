I've added Wheels on the Bus to the song sheet (and whacked Mail Myself to You). I've also changed all the songs except Sanwiches to be in the key of C - and changed the chords so they all use C, F, and G7 (which is easier to finger than G, I think). They are a little lower - but I think still sing-able. 

This simplifies it a lot - just 3 chords for most of these songs. The song sheet starts with the 2-chord songs - and goes to the 3-chord - and ends with Sandwich - since that's in a minor key (I also revised that a little to use the Am - rather than A). I've updated the bootcamp materials to reflect the new chords. 

Both the new song sheet and the bootcamp guide are attached.
