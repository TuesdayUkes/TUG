Hey Tugsters!
Kevin, David, Rich and I have been teaching a quick class at the St. John's library for the last couple of weeks.  The last class is tomorrow night.
Kevin has asked us to come up with a list of links to give to the folks tomorrow night.  He's also suggested that we work on a theme for the Tuesday night group.  How about a twofer?  

1.  For the Weds. night class....  Here are some that I think would be great for them since we've taught them C, F, G7 and could easily expand it to G and Am as well.
https://www.guitarplayerbox.com/list.aspx?type=a&element=deepButton&pList=&chSel=C%2cF%2cG%2cG7&pageNum=1

http://ukulelego.com/lessons/play-hundreds-songs-ukulele-just-4-chords/


2. Regarding a theme for the Tuesday group... I propose that we develop a repertoire of 8 to 10 songs that have only C, F, G, G7, or Am in them.  That way in the future if we're ever going to do another teaching gig we can just pull 'em out and demonstrate them to the class.


My list (in no particular order)
