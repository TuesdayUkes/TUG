Sorry for the late notice - but for the Austin Ukulele Society's 100th meeting TUG will be performing Make Your Chair Hang Low for the chair hanging demo at the end of the meeting. 

We'll stage near the racks by the door - and play through the song once. We'll go through the song a couple of times on Tuesday - and it's pretty simple - so we should not have any problems with it. 

Attached is the song sheet - the tune for this song is Let Your Ears Hang Low - 

As far as an arrangemnet - let's try opening with a tremolo on G, then get into the song. SIng through, and on the last line - slow down, go back to the tremolo , and sing up  the arpeggio for the G chord - eg - Make Your {g} CHAIR {b} HANG {d} LOW! - hold the tremolo at the end. It's more complicated to type than to do - so we'll figure it out on Tuesday

This will be fun!
