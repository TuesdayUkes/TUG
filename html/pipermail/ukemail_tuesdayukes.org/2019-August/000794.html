Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday, August 13 - Hancock Center

Next week we'll do a "dress rehearsal" for the open mic. We'll play standing up using paper on music stands. I'll bring 3 stands and the printed song sheets. We could probably use 1 (or 2) more stands -  we'll try to set up with 3 people per stand to keep the set up a streamlined as possible. We'll do that at the beginning of the meeting. 

Attached is the weekly song PDF file (along with a .archive file for OnSong users). 

Also attached are the MP3s from this week - I think we sound pretty good!

If you have a song you'd like the group to play - send it to tug_info at tuesdayukes.org   or ukemail at tuesdayukes.org   or just bring a copy to the meeting. We'll work it into the rotation.
