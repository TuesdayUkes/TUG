Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday, August 20 - Hancock Center

The AUS open Mic is this Saturday at Kick Butt Coffee - we'll play a little after 5:00 - so be there ready to go by 5:00. If you're signing up to play individually you should probably plan to get there around 4:30. 

Anyone interested in playing at the Baca Center in Round Rock on Friday, September 6 let me know and I'll send out a song book for the set. We'll do 20 minutes or so. We'll figure out car pooling ,etc as we get closer. 

Attached is the weekly song PDF file (along with a .archive file for OnSong users). 

If you have a song you'd like the group to play - send it to tug_info at tuesdayukes.org   or ukemail at tuesdayukes.org   or just bring a copy to the meeting. We'll work it into the rotation.
