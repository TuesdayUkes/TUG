Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday, February 5, 2019 - Hancock Center

We'll be meeting (mostly) in Room 5 - (The big room downstairs)

This week we'll practice Midnight Special in performance mode - so we'll use one end of the room as the "stage" and play with paper and musioc stands. We'll need to figure out how many stands we'll need - so if you have a folding stand handy - bring it in. The Open Mic is Saturday, Feb 16 from 5:00-7:30 at Kick Butt Coffee, 5775 Airport Blvd, Austin, TX, 78752. 

Attached is a song sheet for Midnight Special - I turned off the chord diagrams so it would fit on one sheet.  I'll bring some printed sheets on Tuesday as well. 

Also attached is the weekly song sheet.
