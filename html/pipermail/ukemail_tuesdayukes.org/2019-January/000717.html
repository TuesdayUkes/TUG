Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday January 8- Hancock Center

We'll be meeting (mostly) in Room 5 - (The big room downstairs)

Thanks to everyone who came out for he Uke-Along on New Year's Day - that was a lot of fun. 

Attached is a the first pass at the song book for the next couple of months - it's unfinished - so bring in any songs you'd like to add and we'll put them in the list. 
Also attached is our TUG PUB book - these are pub songs for the post-TUG Brewtorium meeting. (For folks how haven't made it - we've started meeting at the Brewtorium (http://thebrewtorium.com/) after the TUG meeting  - 8:45 or so - for a beer and more playing/singing. On recent vistits they even have given us discounts on beers). The TUG PUB book is a set of pub/drinking songs.  We'll be working these into the weekly schedule. 
And - lastly - we've got the first draft of our Singalong Book - these are songs we'll work up as singalongs for when we play somewhere. There's about 20 songs - so we can pick and choose and have some variety for wherever we play. We'll also work these in as we go. 

All these books are also available on the web site - http://tuesdayukes.org/

6:30 - 7:00 - Flash Class/Practice Group

Quick Chords  - Intro to learning chords - simple chord progressions


7:00 - 8:30ish - TUG

Repetiore Songs

NyQuil Blues
Baby Please Don't Go


Open Mic - If you want to play something for the group

Current Songs 

Midnight Special
Mobile Line
Fancy Beer
I Fear Ikea
The Moonshiner
Take me Home Country Roads

If you have a song you'd like the group to play - send it to tug_info at tuesdayukes.org   or ukemail at tuesdayukes.org   or just bring a copy to the meeting. We'll work it into the rotation.
