Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday, July 16 -  Hancock Center. The weekly song PDF is attached. Also an OnSong archive file (for folks who are using OnSong). 

Anyone interested in playing for the Lamar Senior Center Ice Cream Social ( Tuesday July 23 - 1:00-2:30) - please let me know and I'll send you the song sheets. This is kind of a sing-along - we'll have lyric sheets for the audience - and we'll play from paper/iPads. There are about a dozen tunes in the song list. 

We have 21 songs in the Q3 book - let me know if there's anything you'd like to have the group play - we'll add it to the book. 

We'll be meeting (mostly) in Room 5 - (The big room downstairs)
