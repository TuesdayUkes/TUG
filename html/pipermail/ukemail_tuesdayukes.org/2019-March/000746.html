Invite your friends and neighbors to have a beverage, eat a pretzel, and do some uke-ing and singing at our Tuesday UkeAlong – Tuesday, April 2. 7:00pm at the Brewtorium . We’ve got a dozen tunes in our UkeAlong song book (attached)  and we’ll have printed copies available as well. As usual we’ll have our loaner ukes for the uke-less – and some percussion for the percussive inclined. Bring a music stand if you have one. Hope to see you there!

Tuesday April 2
7:00 - 9:00
Brewtorium
6105 Dillard Circle
