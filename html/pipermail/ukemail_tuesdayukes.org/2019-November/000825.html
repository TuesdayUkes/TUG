Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday November 12- Hancock Center

We won't be meeting the week of Thanksgiving - Tuesday Nov 26. (Hancock Center will be closed for maintenance)
In December we won't be meeting either Dec 24, or Dec 31. 

Our UkeAlong will be Tuesday January 7 at the Central Market North Lamar 7:00-9:00.  We'll vote on the songs in December before the break. 

Just a reminder that the Austin Ukulele Society meets next Thursday - Nov 14. 

Attached is the weekly song PDF file (along with a .archive file for OnSong users). 

If you have a song you'd like the group to play - send it to tug_info at tuesdayukes.org   or ukemail at tuesdayukes.org   or just bring a copy to the meeting. We'll work it into the rotation.
