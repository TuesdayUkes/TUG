Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday, December 3 - Hancock Center

Attached are the songs for the Senior Access holiday singalong. I make a few changes. If there is an instrumental part - there should be a yellow highlight at the top saing "INSTRUMENTAL" - just to give a heads up. I also added the words for the chords for each instrumental part in red - so we don't sing there - just Walter on the Kazoo. Also I added a Kazoo break in Mele Kalikimaka - it's a short tune - I think it will sound nice. Also changed the key for 12 Days to F - just to get the vocal range down a little. We'll go through these at the next meeting. 

Also attached are the holiday chord melodies if you want to give those a whirl. 

Attached is the weekly song PDF file (along with a .archive file for OnSong users). 

If you have a song you'd like the group to play - send it to tug_info at tuesdayukes.org   or ukemail at tuesdayukes.org   or just bring a copy to the meeting. We'll work it into the rotation.
