Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday, October 8 - Hancock Center

Thanks to everyone who came out for our UkeAlong - it was a blast! 
We've got a video up on the website of the group doing ChooChoo Ch'Boogie - http://tuesdayukes.org.
Also, we've updated the events calendar on the web site so it should be easier to keep up to date - and hopefully will be more useful - if you click into an event you'll see all the details - and a map!

Attached is the weekly song PDF file (along with a .archive file for OnSong users). 

If you have a song you'd like the group to play - send it to tug_info at tuesdayukes.org   or ukemail at tuesdayukes.org   or just bring a copy to the meeting. We'll work it into the rotation.
