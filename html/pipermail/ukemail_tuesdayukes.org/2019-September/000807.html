Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday 24 September- Hancock Center

The next UkeAlong is Tuesday October 1 at the Brewtorium - 7:00pm - 9:00pm. There will be no meeting at the Hancock Center that night. The Ukealong song list is attached.

Attached is the weekly song PDF file (along with a .archive file for OnSong users). Also the 2 chord song list is attached. 

If you have a song you'd like the group to play - send it to tug_info at tuesdayukes.org   or ukemail at tuesdayukes.org   or just bring a copy to the meeting. We'll work it into the rotation.
