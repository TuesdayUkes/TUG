Hey Y'all --

I hope everyone is doing well. There's a couple of things coming up this week - 

Austin Ukulele Society - LiveStream Meeting - Thursday, Apr 9,. 7:00pm

Bob and Jen and Lyda are doing a You Tube Livestream of the monthly meeting - this will be a great play-along!

Ukulele Mini-Fest

Craig Chee and Sarah Maisel have put together an online Ukulele festival - workshops, performances, and a uke giveaway. It's 12PM - 6:00pm (our time) on Saturday, April 11. Check out the schedule - there are some great folks 

Lastly, if you have not looked at the TUG web site lately - we have a bunch of new playalong videos - 

http://tuesdayukes.org/index.php/tuesday-ukes-videos/

We're also working on some short strumming videos

http://tuesdayukes.org/index.php/tug-strum-class/

And we're going to work our way through the Spring 2020 Songbook - 

http://tuesdayukes.org/index.php/spring-2020-songbook/

Pick up your uke - and play with us. 

Also - record a video (or audio - MP3, or MP4) of yourself and send me an email with a link (or the file, if it's an audio file) - and I'll post it up on the web site so everyone can play along. 

We also have a weekly on-line open mic - you don't need to play - join in so we can see you! (You can check out unedited videos of those meetings here - http://tuesdayukes.org/index.php/on-line-open-mic/

Take Care - hope to see y'all soon!

Kevin
