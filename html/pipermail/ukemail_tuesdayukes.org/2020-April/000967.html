Since we're sitting at home - this would be a good opportunity to expand your uke skills with some arpeggio practice. Having an arpeggio in your right hand is really handy - if you're tired of the island strum - for instance - you can whip an arpeggio onto just about any song - and it will sound great. 

Here's a great article from Ukulele Magazine by Daniel Ward - stepping us through a really beautiful arpeggio - https://www.ukulelemag.com/uketube/lessons/ukulele-lesson-unlock-big-changes-in-your-playing-with-daniel-wards-meditative-arpeggio. There's also a video in the article where Daniel demo's the technique. 

Paul and I did a little duet based on this arpeggio (and a piece called Home from Daniel's Melodic Meditations book). 

Here's a link to the Sound Cloud https://soundcloud.com/kevin-jolly-438109743/home-arpeggio-meditations-duet

Go arpeggiate!
