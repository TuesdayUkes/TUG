Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday, March 3 - Hancock Center

If you know anyone 50+ interested in the uke we will be having a Uke Camp at the Lamar Senior Activity Center starting March 5 - 9:30am-10:15. The camp is 4 weeks - and we have ukes to use is class. Check out info on the TUG web site - http://tuesdayukes.org/index.php/event/ukulele-camp-lamar-senior-activity-center/2020-03-05/

Attached is the weekly song PDF file (along with a .archive file for OnSong users). 

If you have a song you'd like the group to play - send it to tug_info at tuesdayukes.org   or ukemail at tuesdayukes.org   or just bring a copy to the meeting. We'll work it into the rotation. 

6:30 -7:00 - Two chord songs, and basics
