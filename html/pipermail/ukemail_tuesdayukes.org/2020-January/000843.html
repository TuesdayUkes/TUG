Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday, January 14 - Hancock Center

Welcome Back! It's been almost a month since our last meeting. In the 2 chord warm up from 6:30-7:00 we're going to start to introduce some specific strum patterns. This week we'll work on the Island Strum. Attached is a PDF with a simple intro and a couple of songs which work with that strum. 

We're also starting our new Winter 2020 Songbook - we sent that out last week. 

Attached is the weekly song PDF file (along with a .archive file for OnSong users). 

If you have a song you'd like the group to play - send it to tug_info at tuesdayukes.org   or ukemail at tuesdayukes.org   or just bring a copy to the meeting. We'll work it into the rotation. 

6:30 -7:00 - Two chord songs, and basics
