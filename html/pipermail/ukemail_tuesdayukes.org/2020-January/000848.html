Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday, Feb 5 - Hancock Center

The Austin Ukulele Society Open Mic is Saturday, Feb 22 from 4:00 - 6:00 (or so). We'll being going in the first couple of groups - so we'll want to be there by 3:45 or so. We'll use paper and music stands - we should only need 1 stand for every 2-3 people - so we'll figure that out the week of the open mic. 

Next week we'll record both songs so we can hear what we might need to work on.  I've attached the open mic songs. 

Attached is the weekly song PDF file (along with a .archive file for OnSong users). 

If you have a song you'd like the group to play - send it to tug_info at tuesdayukes.org   or ukemail at tuesdayukes.org   or just bring a copy to the meeting. We'll work it into the rotation. 

6:30 -7:00 - Two chord songs, and basics

7:00 - 8:15-ish - Weekly Songs

8:40 - 9:45 - Post TUG  - Brewtorium (6015 Dillard Cir) - for anyone interested - we get a beer and play and sing some more.
