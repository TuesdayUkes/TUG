All,

 

Like the rest of you, I can't believe Kevin is gone. 

 

We frequently exchanged emails about a whole range of things...music,
ukuleles, exotic instruments, instruments we wish we could buy and
variations on that theme.

uke as he put a tremendous amount of time in various ukulele-related
