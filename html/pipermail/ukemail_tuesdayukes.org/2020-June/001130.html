Hello,

Walter, I am pleased to read you are getting out of the hospital today and healing well. Indeed, that is great news. I hope your health journey allows for continued strength and recovery.

Everyone, thank you for sharing your sentiments, feelings, and thoughts about how Kevin chose to live his life - with great generosity of talent, humor, and time.  Reading through your perspectives has validated my own shock, sadness, and grief. Thank you for your expressions, it’s reassuring to know we are not alone.

The world was made a better place with Kevin. He meant a lot to me, it was always comforting to know he was there to answer a question, offer his assistance, or simply just to chit and chat about ukuleles, motorcycles trips, suitcase drums, cardboard harp building...., with our shared interests, this list could go on and on.....,

I will miss him greatly.

Please take care of yourselves and the ones with whom you all share your lives.

With love,

Nancy
