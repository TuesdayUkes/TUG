Hey Folks --

I hope everyone's doing well. Paul, Marty, Walter and I have been messing around with various ways for us to all play together - while remaining separate - and nothing has really worked out to replicate the group experience.  We're going to try an online open mic using Zoom - which looks promising - we'll send out details once we get it figured out. 

As for our weekly songs - I've cut the list down a little - and over the next couple of days I'll put together a video play-along - it's not ideal - but it's something. I'll update everyone when it's online. 

I'm going to try to spend some time on the web site adding the various play-along/singalong videos we're working on - so they will all be in one place. I'll also try to pull together a big PDF of TUG songs  - and post that up there as well. 

If you have a video or audio recording you'd like to share with the group  - send it out to ukemail at tuesdayukes.org - we can all use a little ukulele uplifting.
