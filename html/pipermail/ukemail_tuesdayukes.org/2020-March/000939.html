Hey Folks --

Here's the Spring 2020 Songbook - we'll pull songs for our weekly meetings from here - and from the 2 chord song list. 

There's 40 songs this spring - which is a bunch - so we may not get through them all before Summer. We'll start next week - and see how it goes. 

Here's a You Tube playlist for the songs - some of them have a couple of versions.

https://tinyurl.com/wswoa2u

As always, once we start playing these songs together things will change - this is the starting point - the updated songs will be in our weekly emails. 

Attached is a PDF and a .archive file for folks who use OnSong.
