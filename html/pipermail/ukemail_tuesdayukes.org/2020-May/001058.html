Here is the CC Rider video lesson from Del Rey. 

https://youtu.be/SlfcfASwDFw

I've also done a rough transcript of the lyrics and basic chords which is attached. 

Here's the plan. 

1) Use the video - and work on what's interesting to you  - there's plenty! (see below for links to specific section, this is also on the PDF)
2) Keep a set of questions - could be about the tune, techniques, blues, ukuleles, jug bands, etc
3) On Wednesday, May 27 (7:00 CDT), we'll get together with Del on Zoom - and have a review/question/answer session

I think this would be a good candidate for a group recording (once we get it under our belts).
