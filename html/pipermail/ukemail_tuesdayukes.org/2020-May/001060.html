If you're overwhelmed by Del Rey's CC Rider video - here's one way to get going. Do as little or as much as you want - 

1) Just focus on the main body of the song - skip the intro (for now) - https://youtu.be/SlfcfASwDFw?t=723
2) Use the chords you are already familiar with - https://www.dropbox.com/s/27n9ozay6mjznpq/CC%20Rider%20-%20Del.pdf?dl=0
Then - if you want --
3) Add the turnaround - play it at beginning and between each verse - https://youtu.be/SlfcfASwDFw?t=763
4) Once you have the turaround and the chords, work on the Walk Up  - https://youtu.be/SlfcfASwDFw?t=436
5) And finally - see if you can add in the Riff - https://youtu.be/SlfcfASwDFw?t=633

That would give you a great version of the tune which mixes strumming and licks - without having to go up the neck

On the alternate C chord (0787 - G shape on the 7th fret) - if you're having trouble making the stretch to 0585 - try sliding the whole shape down to the 5th fret (0575) - and using your little finger on the 8th fret. You don't need to pick up your third finger - just leave it in place. Here's a little video showing it really slow - it's probably easier to see than explain. 

 https://youtu.be/HSNqDRWFvUY 


If anyone has any other hints or tips - shoot an email to the list - I know I could use the help!
