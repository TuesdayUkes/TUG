Hi, friends! 
We're excited for you to participate in Austin Ukulele Society's September meeting collaborative video! Please review the notes below carefully and let us know if you have any questions. :)

We invite you to make either an audio or video recording of yourself playing "Can't Fight This Feeling" along with Bob.  Our great friend from TUG, Marty Porter, will once again compile everyone's contributions into a collaborative video that we'll publish on our YouTube channel and share on Facebook and our website. 

Here are the details:
   
   - Grab a copy of Bob's rendition of "Can't Fight This Feeling" from the shared folder below (it's named "Cant_Fight_This_Feeling_AUS_PLAY_ALONG.mp4"): https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1H1cATSfcG8KYvlDdX7KkbCcfihW3kBRM.  The play along video includes:
   
   - A click/drum track throughout to make it easy to follow the beat.
   - A visual countdown to help you start off at the same time. There's another visual countdown at the end to signal the final strum.
   - The slides from the meeting presentation, to make it easy to follow the chords and lyrics.
   
   - Record audio or video of yourself playing along. Though our strong preference is that you record yourself on the whole song, it's also okay if it's just one verse/chorus (if you prefer it) - but please record for at least a minute. Recording yourself on the whole song increases the likelihood that you'll be in the final video. If you record part of the song only, please specify which part you record.  
   - Please only record your part of the audio and not Bob's, by listening to Bob's through earbuds or headset.
   - As much as you can, try to have yourself centered in the frame (by the way, landscape is preferred over portrait). 
   - Choose a quiet enough place and try to place yourself at a suitable proximity to the mic. Please take a listen to your recording after you're done to see if you hear yourself clearly; this will help us hear you in the compilation. :)
Please name your file "Lastname_Firstname - full (or) partial.mp4" (or mp3 for audio).  Upload your file on Marty's shared Google Drive here: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1H1cATSfcG8KYvlDdX7KkbCcfihW3kBRM
Please submit your files by Sunday, September 27th, at 8pm - and when you upload it, please send a note to us (austinukes at gmail.com) and Marty (mlp at flash.net). 
If you have any questions, please let us know. :)
Thanks, friends!
Best,Jen (and Marty and Bob!)
