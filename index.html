About Our Meetings

We average 10 to 12 people in person at the Hancock Recreation Center and 3 to 4 people online via Zoom. We welcome players of all skill levels, and beginners are always welcome! Our meetings follow an open mic format, but everyone is free to play along.

Members can submit songs a few days before the meeting to share with the group. Songs are formatted for both in-person projection and Zoom viewing. While submitting songs isn't required, it's a great way to participate!

To make playing easier for newer players, we choose favorite songs to practice each week. These are posted on the website before the meeting so you have time to practice.

Have questions? Email us at TUG+owner@groups.io. We're a friendly, informal group and would love to have you join us!
