Looking forward to seeing you at CM tonight.  Thanks again to Kevin for
creating and keeping our website current.

I know this month's theme is "holiday" and I'll try to come up with a good
story for what I have been working on this week.  Basically, it's a
modified claw hammer.  I'll try to demo it tonight but in the meantime if
anyone is interested here is how it goes:


   1. Thumb plucks “c” string.
   2. Down strum all four strings.
   3. Index finger pluck “a” string.
   4. Thumb plucks “g” string.
   5. Down strum all four strings.
