I know this month's theme is "holiday" and I'll try to come up with a good story for what I have been working on this week.  Basically, it's a modified claw hammer.  I'll try to demo it tonight but in the meantime if anyone is interested here is how it goes:
   
   - Thumb plucks “c” string.
   - Down strum all four strings.
   - Index finger pluck “a” string.
   - Thumb plucks “g” string.
   - Down strum all four strings.
