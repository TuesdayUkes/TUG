I included a pdf and a mp3 example.  The "p" means thumb,  "i" is index,
and "m" is middle.  The important thing to note is m only plays the 1st
string, m only plays the 2nd string, and the thumb alternates on the bass
strings.

The basic right hand pattern is:  "together, inside, outside, inside"
