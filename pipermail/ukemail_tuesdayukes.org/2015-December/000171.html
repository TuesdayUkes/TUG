This is a great site featuring Hawaiian musicians, primarily (hope the link
works).  A number of great ukulele players, as well as other great music,
too.

https://www.youtube.com/user/hisessionsshow

Each artist or group comes in and as part of their session does a
short "introduction" video and then 4 or 5 music videos.  The videos can be
watched individually (or set to play randomly), or you can select an
"episode" and watch a particular artist's full session.  I believe there
are also some full length concert videos.
