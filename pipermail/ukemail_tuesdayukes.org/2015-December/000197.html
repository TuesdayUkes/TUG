My non-ukulele reprobate compadres are planning a #3 Bus Beer Crawl for 
January 2.

We will convene at The Draught House (4112 Medical Parkway) at 3:00 on 
Saturday Jan 2. There will be beers - then we'll move north - afoot 
and/or on the #3 (or #803) bus. There are a number of stop with either 
good beer or a good venue - Pint House Pizza, Billy's on Burnet, the 
Growler Room, Lala's Little Nugget, The Aristocrat (formerly Poodle 
Dog), Ginny's Little Longhorn, Little Woodrows, C Hunts Ice House, etc. 
There's also a bunch of places to eat along the way - so no one will go 
hungry. There is no set itinerary - but we plan to end the crawl early 
in the evening at Austin Beer Works - 3009 Industrial Terrace - they do 
a nice tasting - $10 for a pint glass and 3 fills.

I was thinking it might be fun to get a contingent of ukes - and maybe 
subject the folks at each venue to a song or two - Time Warp comes to 
mind as one good choice. Regardless - there will be plenty of good beers 
and questionable company.

So, I'm throwing this out to TUG - come crawl with your ukes!

Kevo


Saturday January 2
3:00 PM
