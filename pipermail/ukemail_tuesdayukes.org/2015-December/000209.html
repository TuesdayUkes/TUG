Well, they took snow out of the forecast for Saturday - but it's still 
predicted to me cold and wet - perfect weather to stand at a bus stop!

We're still going to start up at 3:00 at the Draught House - and plan to 
end up at Austin Beer Works by 5:30ish. I will bring some song sheets 
for BEER BEER BEER. Here's a list of potential stops along the way, and 
the #3 route map and schedule. You can buy a day pass on the bus - or 
there's some kind of intelligent phone app as well. You don't have to 
ride the bus to participate.


3:00 Start At:

Draught House
4112 Medical Parkway
http://www.draughthouse.com/
More than 70 taps, decent house beers


Likely Interim Locations Along Bus Route #3

Pint House Pizza
4729 Burnet Road
http://pinthousepizza.com/
Exceelent House Beers, 40 or so guest taps, decent Pizza

Billy's On Burnet
2105 Hancock Dr (around 5100 Block of Burnet)
~30 taps, decent beers, burgers, pub food

Ginny's Little Longhorn
5434 Burnet
http://thelittlelonghornsaloon.com/
$1 Lone Star Drafts 5:00-6:00, Happy Hour band TBA, Pickled suasage

Little Woodrows
5424 Burnet
~20 taps, plenty of Frat Boys

The Aristocrat
6507 Burnet
https://www.facebook.com/AristocratLoungeATX
Previously known as The Poodle Dog. Beers - now served in bottles 
because the clientele got too old to hit each other with them.

Lalas Little Nugget
2207 Justin Ln (Burnet & Justin)
https://foursquare.com/v/lalas-little-nugget/40b13b00f964a520bdf31ee3
Beers, Xmas decorations, excellent jukebox

Conan's Pizza
2438 West Anderson Ln (Burnet & Anderson)
http://conanspizza.com/
3 Beers - fat pizza - Barbarians.

C Hunts Ice House
9611 McNeil Rd (Burnet & NcNeil)
Beers in Bottles. Old reprobates - bu the paint shop so plenty of paint 
fumes, Tables under grape arbors.

End at:

Austin Beer Works
3009 Industrial Terrace Suite 150 (back behind the CocaCola plant, kind 
of hidden in the back of Austin Opera - look for the cars)
$10 for a glass and 3 fills - some kind of food truck in attendance - 
only open until 7:00 - so need to hit it before then.

We'll try to hit ABW by 5:30ish and close them down (at 7:00).

If people have the bad sense to continue riding/drinking we'll decamp 
back south to somewhere warm.
