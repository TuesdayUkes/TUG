I've made a few revisions to the song sheet for Ukulele. Fixed a couple of typos, and added an NC in the verses. I also added a pause and stage direction where we would look at Bob and Jen  - we can decide if that makes sense once we do it a couple of times. 


For the strumming patterns. On the refrain - I think it might be good to use your thumb with a simple down strum on the beat (1-2-3-4-5-6) - I think it will quiet/soften that a little.
At the end of the refrain, on the last "LE", we might try just one down stroke with the thumb on the first beat, then U-D-U with either the thumb or finger, on 4-5-6. Then we would stay with that pattern on the C-Am-A into the next verse - it sounds more complicated than it is. We can talk about it when we meet - and see if it would work.
