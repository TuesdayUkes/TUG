Happy Holiday TUGsters!  Soon the big day will be part of Christmas past,
and we'll all be relaxing from the shopping craze.  Before you put those
Holiday Songs away and get ready for the New Year, let's get together and
play some tunes.  We have plenty of space, so bring your significant others
along and we'll include them in the fun!  Don't forget your favorite music
books and songs to share.

For some of you, we are on the far side of the moon, but please consider
taking the trek and we'll reward your efforts with a nice dinner and a fine
drink selection.  We live near the Dell Diamond (baseball park), on the
East side of Round Rock.
