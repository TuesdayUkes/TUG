I'd suggest that we learn Midnight on the Water - this is an instrumental piece - which we did at the recent workshop with Bob. There are 3 uke parts, plus chords - I set up Bob's arrangement in Guitar Pro. I asked Bob if we could steal his arrangement and he said sure - and he'd like to see TUG do it at AUS one month. 


So, here's a You Tube of some guys sitting around the kitchen playing the tune - give a listen


https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=67akVfmHn6w



And I've attached the separate parts as PDfs - and the GP6 version with all the parts together. 


The Uke 1 part goes up the neck a bit - but the other 2 parts are all in 1st position. I think the chords are on the Uke 1 part. 


This would take a little bit of work - but it would sound really good, I think  - and it would be neat to have an instrumental piece. 


Anyhow - take a look - give a listen. 


Kevo
