Jack is hosting another Ukulele Support Group meeting at Ruiz Library on Saturday May 14 from 2:00 to 4:00

Jack and Mardi ( and some of us ) will be playing at the YMCA on June 3 - do we’ll make a run through our sets - and we’ll have plenty of time for anyone else who wants to play to get up and rip off some Ukulele madness. 

It’s a great room, and a great group. It’s an excellent place to preview/practice playing in front of a (small/supportive) audience with no sirens or motorcycles. 

Come out and play or just show up to watch - we need more people!
