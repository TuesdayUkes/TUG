Here's a song for us to work up as a closer - Happy Trails to You.

The LEAD PDF has the chords, melody and words. The Uke1, Uke2, and Bass 
PDFs have the notation and TABS. There's a short intro, and them a 
chorus - we can sing and play  the first time through, then an 
instrumental the second time, and finish the past time through singing 
again. The Uke parts are notated for Low G - but will work just fine 
with high-G ukes - you'll just use the fingering shown on the TABS.

Here's a You tube with Roy and Dale singing

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XcYsO890YJY

We'll go through the lead sheet at the next TUG - and maybe make a pass 
through the TAB parts.

Is anyone has Sibelius, or Avid Scorch let me know and I'll send you the 
.sib file.

Kevin
