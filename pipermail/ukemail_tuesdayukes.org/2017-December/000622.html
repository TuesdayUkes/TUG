Our fellow tugster - Raj - will be playing with the Austin Classic Guitar Adult Ensemble this Sunday at the new Central Library downtown. 

It's free! - and starts at 2:00 - here's some more info

https://www.austinclassicalguitar.org/event/acg-community-ensembles-at-austin-central-library/

Hear some great music - and check out the new Library if you haven't been - it's gorgeous.

Come on out!
