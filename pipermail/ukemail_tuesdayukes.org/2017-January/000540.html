We'll have our next iTUG meeting next Tuesday - Jan 31 - at the Hancock 
Recreation center. If we're not in one of our usual spots - ask at the 
front desk - we've been moving around lately.

I've got some new parts up on the Dropbox site.

For the TUG Boogie in G I added the notes for just the G-C, C-E, and E-A 
strings (along with the whole set). We might go around the room and have 
each uke player just use notes from one of those sets of strings. It is 
just a way to reduce the complexity a little.

Uke2 to Uke 3. The new part is a harmony part - with a simplified rhythm.
