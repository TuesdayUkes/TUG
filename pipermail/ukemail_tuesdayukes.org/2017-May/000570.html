Here's a tremolo study from Daniel Ward - you play the first note of each 4 note set with your thumb, and the next three with your index , middle , and ring fingers.  You can also play it index-middle-index - but you will not be able to play it as fast (ultimately)

Play really, really, really slow to start. Work on getting all the notes the same length before you try to speed up at all.
