Start the New Year with some Uke Music!  The Tuesday Ukulele Group  is hosting a New Year Tuesday Use-Along - 2:00-4:00 At the Central Market, 4100 North Lamar.  We'll have loaner ukes for the uke-less, and shakers and cowbell for the percussionists. Attached if our song book for the festa - and we'll have printed copies available as well. If the weather is nice we'll be out on the patio - and it's it's not we'll be inside the cafe - just listen for the ukes. If you have a music stand - that could be handy. We'll have a sign up sheet for a little open mic - so if you've got something you'd like to play for an audience - sign up!

You can get the latest info at the TUG web site - http://tuesdayukes.org

Hope to see you there!
