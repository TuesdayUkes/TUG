Here's the first section of Lilting Banshee with the High G and Low G Tabs. I've also added the chords. 

For folks who don't want to play the melody they can work on the chords. To start, just strum on the first and the fourth beat (ONE two three TWO two three). We are practicing at about 40 beats per minute - so that would be a good place to start. The chords - like the rest of the song - are challenging. Once the rhythm players get the chord changes down we can look at maybe adding in an arpeggio - but this would sound great with just the strums.
