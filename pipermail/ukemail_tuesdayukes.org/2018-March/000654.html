Aaron Kein has a new Fingerstyle book out - 

10 Etudes for Fingerstyle Ukulele 

http://quietamericanmusic.com/storestore/10-fingerstyle-etudes-for-ukulele-digital-download

It’s kind of a follow-on to his Fingerstyle Ukulele book - the Etudes are well graded (less hard -> harder). 

A really good beginner fingerstyle book  (at least I really like it) - is Rob McKillop’s 20 Easy Fingerstyle Studies for the Ukulele. (https://robmackillop.net/ukulele/) He’s a big proponent of campanella style - so this is really much better with a high-g uke. The studies are really interesting and well-graded. This is 4 finger (p-i-m-a) fingerstyle - which is a little different than Aaron’s 3-finger (p-i-m) approach. Rob also has a follow-on  - 20 Progressive Fingerstyle Studies for Uke. His book are all published by Mel Bay - so you can get them from Amazon, etc. He has several really good repertoire books  - and even a book of songs for the machete - https://robmackillop.net/machete/
