Hanock Center is doing mainetance next week - so no TUG (May 15). Our next meeting will be Tuesday May 22. We'll focus on our songs for St John's Library since that is coming up that Friday - St John's Library, 3:15pm (be there at 3:00). 

The Uke-along at Oscar Blues went well - we took our loaner ukes - and a an innocent bystander got sucked into the uke-vortex - she even showed up at AUS on Thursday! Weather was great - there were $2 beers - and the Oscar Blues management said we could come back anytime - so we'll probably make that a regular thing. 

We took our new rack (thanks to Marty) and five loaner ukes to the Austin Ukulele Society meeting this week   - four of them were picked up and used during the meeting - which is great for the first appearance. Lots of folks came up afterward to check them out - and it looks like the demand might exceed the supply. We'll be using them at St John's for the Uke petting zoo as well. Hopefully they'll get played alot going  forward. 

I'll try to get a song sheet together for St John's before the next meeting and send it out (and I'll bring hard copies to the meeting as well). 

In June we'll go back to semi-monthly theme - so be thinking about a theme for June - we'll talk about it at the meeting. 

We're also going to change up the format a little. We'll have 6 or so songs that we'll work on each month - and we'll play a couple of those at the begiing and end of each meeting. After that we'll have an "open mic" for anyone who wants to practice playing in front of folks, then we'll play anything someone brings, and if there is nothing new - we'll finish on our monthly songs. We'll put the songs we work on in a book for our uke-alongs - and performances like St John's. 

Last - and least - I've updated the web site -  http:// tuesdayukes.org - I've got our calendar right up at the top - and I'll try to keep it up to date. 

Whew - that's it - see y'all in a couple of weeks!
