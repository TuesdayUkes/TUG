We'll have our Flash Class again from 6:30 to 7:00pm. If no new folks show up we'll be working on the tremolo strum for El Condor Pasa, and the rhumba strum for You Are My Sol Del Luz. 

We'll start at 7:00 with our two repertoire songs for this week: C&H Sugar and Baby Please Don't Go. 

We have a new Xmas song to try - I Want a Hippopotamus for Christmas - this has been added to the song sheet (http://tuesdayukes.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/TugQ418.pdf) - it might be a better choice for the group than Chipmunks. 

Then we'll work on:

Nyquil Blues
Waltzing Matilda
Wagon Wheel
Miles and Miles of Texas

If anyone has anything they want to bring - email to the list (ukemail at tuesdayukes.org) or bring a copy on Tuesday. 

See you Tuesday, Ukes!
