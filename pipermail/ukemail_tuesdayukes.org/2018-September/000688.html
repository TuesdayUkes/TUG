We're having another Uke-Along at Oscar Blues (https://www.oskarblues.com/breweries/austin/) next Tuesday at 7:00. 

It's $2.00 canned beers night - they also have brewed soft drinks and coffee - and free tap water! 

We've got a dozen or so songs to play and sing - the song sheet is attached - and we'll have some printed copies that night as well. We'll also bring our loabner ukes - so if you have a friend or neighbor you want to infect with the uke virus bring them along. We've also got some shakers, cowbell, and claves if you know someone more inclined to the percussion section.  We'll also have a few music stands - but you might want to bring one if you have one handy. 

If it's raining the night of, we'll meet at Central Market on Lamar (https://centralmarket.com/austin-north-lamar-events/) - just look (and listen) for ukes. 

Hope to hear you there!
