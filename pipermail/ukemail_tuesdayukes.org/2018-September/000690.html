We have our Uke-along at Central Market this week because of the rain - and had a great time - we had a whole table of ladies do a Flash Class - and had dancers and walk ups while we played. But - it's too confusing to have an alternative location for bad weather - so we're going to be looking for one place where we can play inside (and out). Along those lines we'll be having our Uke Alongs quarterly in 2019 - The first Tuesday in Jan April July Oct - and that means that the first one will be Tuesday January 1. That could be fun. We'll shoot out info as we get it organized. 

Here's the start of our song book for the last quarter of 2018. Please send me any songs you want added - or bring them to a meeting. I'll also be posting this on the web site so you can always grab one there. By the end of Dec we should have 12-16 songs. 

Another thing we're going to work on is a Grand Jam - we'll try to get the various Ukulele Groups that have formed around town together on some Saturday for a big Uke Jam - we'll let you know when that comes together. 

Austin Parks and Rec has asked us to send out this survey to all participants. If you don't want to do it online you can pick one up at Hancock. 

https://www.surveymonkey.com/r/HSBXT69

We also are going back to the roll sheet we used to fill out - bring your card with you the next time you come out so I can get your ID# - I'll put together a pre-printed roll sheet so we can just check where we're there  (rather than fill out all that stuff every time)
