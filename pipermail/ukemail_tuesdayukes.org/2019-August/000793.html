Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday, August 6- Hancock Center

Next week we'll rehearse our open mic tunes sitting down using the projector - and save the dress rehearsal for the week of the show. Attached is a PDF for Everybody Loves My Baby - with some additional rehearsal notes. We'll focus on Everybody Loves next week. Also attached are the MP3's from this week's meeting - they sound pretty good!

Also attached is the weekly song PDF file (along with a .archive file for OnSong users). 

If you have a song you'd like the group to play - send it to tug_info at tuesdayukes.org   or ukemail at tuesdayukes.org   or just bring a copy to the meeting. We'll work it into the rotation. 

6:30 -7:00 - Two chord songs, and basics
