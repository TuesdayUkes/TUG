Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday Feb 12 - Hancock Center

We'll be meeting (mostly) in Room 5 - (The big room downstairs)

We'll practice Midnight Special in our performance mode again.  The MP3 of our last go round is attached (no bass on this one). I'll try to remember to pull a recording next week as well. 

The Open Mic is Saturday, Feb 16, from 5:00 - 7:30. Kick Butt Coffee - 5775 Airport Blvd. 

From 6:30 - 6:55 we'll do  Uke Camp - it's a basic intro to the uke for folks new to the instrument. This will be the first camp with our new booklet - so anyone interested is welcome (and we'd appreciate any feedback). 

We'll start reviewing closers for the end of the meeting - tonight Goodnight Irene.
