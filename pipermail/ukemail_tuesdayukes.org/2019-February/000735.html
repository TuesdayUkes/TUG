Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday, Feb 26 - Hancock Center

We'll be meeting (mostly) in Room 5 - (The big room downstairs)

This week on the repetoire songs we'll be playing Don't Mess With My TooT Toot - which is a nice bouncy 2 chord song. But we'll only project the lyrics - so we can work on hearing the chord changes. 

Our closer this week is a great Woody Guthrie tune - Dusty Old Dust. 


6:30 - 7:00 - Flash Class/Practice Group

Triplet strum - Two/Three Chord Songs


7:00 - 8:30ish - TUG

Repetiore Songs

Don't Mess With My Toot Toot
Nyquil Blues

Open Mic - If you want to play something for the group

Take Me Home Country Roads
