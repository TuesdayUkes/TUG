Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday, Jan 22 - Hancock Center 

We'll be meeting (mostly) in Room 5 - (The big room downstairs - but check at the desk - sometimes they let us meet upstairs with the grownups.)
For anyone who's interested we;re planning to do  Midnight Special at the Austin Ukulele Society open Mic on Feb 16. We'll keep Midnight Special on the list for the next few sessions to get it ready. This is (like everything we do) completely optional - so don't sweat it if you're not interested in doing the open mic  - you'll just get really good on Midmight Special. 
I've attached a PDF with the songs for this week (the formatting is not the best so if you print it you'll end up with some short pages).
