Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday June 18 - Hancock Center

We'll be meeting (mostly) in Room 5 - (The big room downstairs)

The July UkeAlong is Tuesday July 2 - the song sheet is attached here.

On Thursday June 27 we'll have a tech session on using the iPAD for the uke - mostly focused on using onSong to organize and display song sheets - but also to answer questions and figure stuff our in general. It will be at the North Village Branch Library - 2505 Steck Ave - from 2:00 - 3:30.  Bring your iPad, uke, and questions. 

Also attached is our weekly song sheet.
