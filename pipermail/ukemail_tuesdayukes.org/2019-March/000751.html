Tuesday, April 2 - UKEALONG!

We'll meet at the Brewtorium (6015 Dillard Circle - http://thebrewtorium.com/),  7:00pm.  Look (or listen)  for the ukes - it's one big room so we should not be hard to find. 

Bring a music stand if you have one handy. If you have any Uke-curious family, friends, or neighbors - bring them along. The more singers the better - and we'll have our loaner ukes for anyone who wants to give that a shot - and also claves, shakers,  a hand drum, and cowbell - for the perussion inclined. 

We'll have printed song books - if you need one - (no projector). The PDF of the song book is attached. 

See you there!
