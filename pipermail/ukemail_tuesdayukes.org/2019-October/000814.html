I always get dizzy when I look at these chord descriptions, so maybe I'm
reading these backwards, but I think you have a couple of typos:
Em 0347 should be 0437
C 0334 should be 0433
G 0577 should be 0775
D 0556 should be 0655
A 0799 should be 0997

Also, I think it works well in a single position if some people play the
top three strings and the rest play the bottom three strings (especially
with a low G uke).
Both parts in this video are played on the usual chord shapes, but on
different strings:
https://youtu.be/VDLfKpvlWvU
