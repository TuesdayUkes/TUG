Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday Oct 15 - Hancock Center

We'll be doing a singalong for Senior Access on Friday, Dec 13 - sometime between 9:30am and noon. The venue will be a church in Pflugerville. I'll send out the song sheet in the next couple of weeks - and we'll work the song into our weekly TUG meeting in place of the repertoire song each week. 

Attached is the weekly song PDF file (along with a .archive file for OnSong users). 

If you have a song you'd like the group to play - send it to tug_info at tuesdayukes.org   or ukemail at tuesdayukes.org   or just bring a copy to the meeting. We'll work it into the rotation.
