For anyone interested we'll be doing a Holiday Performance on Friday, Dec 13 at Pflugerville United Methodist Church. 

Here's the info from our web calendar - http://tuesdayukes.org/index.php/event/senior-access-holiday-party/

This is for Senior Access which provides transportation to seniors in Northern Travis and Southern Williamson counties. 

We'll have lyrics sheets for the audience for 4 of the tunes - 12 Days, Rudolph, Christmas Island, and We Wish You. Over the next few weeks at TUG we'll work these songs in as the first two each meeting. We'll try to finalize who's going to play at the December 3 meeting. 

Attached is a PDF and .archive file for OnSong users. We'll use music stands and paper at the performance. 

Here's the set list:
