Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday , September 10 - Hancock Center

Our next Ukealong will be Tuesday, October 1 at the Brewtorium - 7:00 - 8:30ish. We'll vote on the songs for the songbook in the next couple of weeks - we'll send an email when the voting opens. 

Attached is the weekly song PDF file (along with a .archive file for OnSong users). We've added a PDF (and .archive file) for the 2 chord songs as well - we won't get to all of them on the list - but the 2 chord songs will be pulled from this list. 

If you have a song you'd like the group to play - send it to tug_info at tuesdayukes.org   or ukemail at tuesdayukes.org   or just bring a copy to the meeting. We'll work it into the rotation.
