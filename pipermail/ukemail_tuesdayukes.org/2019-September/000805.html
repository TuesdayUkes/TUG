Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday, September 17 - Hancock Center

Here's the link to the survey for the UkeAlong Songs - please have your votes in by Friday - I'll total them this weekend. 

https://forms.gle/91MDPkGZDqnw3v5y8

The next UkeAlong is Tuesday, October 1 at the Brewtorium. 

Attached is the weekly song PDF file (along with a .archive file for OnSong users). 

If you have a song you'd like the group to play - send it to tug_info at tuesdayukes.org   or ukemail at tuesdayukes.org   or just bring a copy to the meeting. We'll work it into the rotation.
