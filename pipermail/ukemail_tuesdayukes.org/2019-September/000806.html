Our next UkeAlong is Tuesday October 1 at the Brewtorium - from 7:00pm - 9:00pm (or so). We'll have loaners and percussion so invite your ffamily and friends!

Here's the song list - these are the top 12 songs from the Ukealong voting. I'll also note that Tie Me Kangaroo Down got the lowest score ever - so it has the distinction of being the most hated song we've done!

Attached is the songbook for the UkeAlong - some of the songs have verses removed to make them fit on a single page - but otherwise they're pretty much as we played them in TUG. We'll have printed books the night of the event. (also attached is a .archive file for OnSong users).
