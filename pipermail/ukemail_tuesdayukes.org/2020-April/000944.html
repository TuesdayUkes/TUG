We've set up a regular weekly Online Open Mic on Zoom for Tuesdays at 7:00pm for the duration of this medical emergency. 

Each week we'll open with a song and close with a song - and after the meeting the folks interested in staying around to play some more can do that. See the attached PDF for the 2 group songs for this week. 

The way the Open Mic works is we just go around the screen and ask folks if they would like to play something. If they do, we put the spotlight on them (they become the focus), and then we mute the group - including the performer. Then we unmute the performer - and they play. At the end we unmute everyone. It's a little kludgy - but it works.

We also record the Open Mic session and send it out - and post it to the web page - so if you want to see what they're about - you can check them out at http://tuesdayukes.org/index.php/on-line-open-mic/
