Hey Y'all --

Here's a few things you could play with this week - the PDFs are attached to the email. 

Spring 2020 Songbook - Grandma's Feather Bed and London Homesick Blues, two great songs from the 70's that are easy to play, and easy to sing. 

Strum Class - the Back Beat Strum - it's polka time!

Smile - This is a great rendition from Paul. The PDF is linked in the You Tube description

2 Chord Songs - there are a bunch of 2 chord songs in this video

Remember you can always slow down (or speed up) videos in You Tube - just click on the little gear icon at the bottom right of the You Tube screen (settings) - snd select playback speed. 

Check the web page http://tuesdayukes.org/ - new videos and stuff are being added just about every day. 

Tomorrow night is our Online Open Mic - we'll send out an email tomorrow with a link and copies of the songs we'll do as a group. 

Everyone take care - and keep strumming!
