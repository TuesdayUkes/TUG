For folks interested in something different from strumming on the ukulele

The Jumping Flea Arpeggiator - a free book with 30 etudes, plus audio files - this is a pretty comprehensive set up etudes -every string, all over the fretboard - 
https://mammothgardens.com/arpeggiator/


Samantha Muir Ukulele - Samantha has a bunch of free arrangements for uke pieces and etudes - and several really nice etude books -  They're all available in PDF format
https://samanthamuir.com/ukulele/


PDF Minstrel has a tin of free chord melody arrangement of classical, folk, and pop tunes
https://pdfminstrel.wordpress.com/2-standard-high-g-ukulele-pdfs/


John's Etudes - this isa book of original ukulele etudes by John Watson - he's released it to the public domain - which is great for us. A number of these etudes look back at the Hawaiian origins of the uke - which is really interesting
https://www.dropbox.com/s/v4ehgoigt2rjb6a/Ukulele%20Etudes.pdf?dl=0

Go play your uke!
