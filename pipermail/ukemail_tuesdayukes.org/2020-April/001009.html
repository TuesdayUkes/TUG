Hey Y'all --

I hope everyone is hanging in - here's some uke stuff to keep you going - For a change of pace - go in the backyard and play loud enough to annoy your neighbor's dog. 

The Roll Strum - This is a great strum to have in your toolkit - and you can practice it without a uke! The sheet is attached - and here's a little video

https://youtu.be/iR9TdYwNzuM

Spring Song Sheet - #6 - South of the Border and Mister Sandman - two great tunes - Mister Sandman has a neat picking part. (Note - in Mister Sandman there is a Dm7 chord - which OnSong refuses to display correctly - the chord is 2213 - a Dm with your little finger on the 3rd fret of the 1st string. ) The song sheet is attached -and here's a video - with the usual sloppy production values!

https://youtu.be/OIaLBKlojpY 

And you can check out all the Spring Songbook videos on the TUG web page - http://tuesdayukes.org/index.php/spring-2020-songbook/

We'll be having our weekly Online Open Mic tomorrow night at 7:00pm - you don't have to play anything - just come hang out with us - we love to see everyone
