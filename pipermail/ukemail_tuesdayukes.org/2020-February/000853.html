Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday, Feb 18 - Hancock Center

The AUS Open Mic at Kick Butt Coffee is Saturday, Feb 22, 3:30 - 6:00pm. 
Here's the info with the address, map, etc - http://tuesdayukes.org/index.php/event/austin-ukulele-society-open-mic/

We'll need a couple of music stands for our "dress rehearsal" next week - so if you have one handy bring it along.

I'll do one-sheet print versions of the songs and bring them next week - I'll also send out PDFs once I get them done. 

Attached is the weekly song PDF file (along with a .archive file for OnSong users). 

If you have a song you'd like the group to play - send it to tug_info at tuesdayukes.org   or ukemail at tuesdayukes.org   or just bring a copy to the meeting. We'll work it into the rotation.
