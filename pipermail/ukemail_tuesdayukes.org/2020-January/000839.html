The first Tuesday UkeAlong of 2020 is coming up next Tuesday. January 7 - from 7:00-8:30 ish - at the Central Market on North Lamar. 

We're going to use our projector to project the song sheets - so no music stands! As usual we'll have our loaners, and a big bag o'percussion implements - so bring out your uke-curious (or not) friends and neighbors. You'll find us on the porch at the Central Market Cafe - just look for ukes. Come a little early and eat - or have a beverage. We'll start the songbook at 7:00 - and take a break in the middle somewhere.

Here's the song list for this UkeAlong - and the song sheet is attached (along with a .archive file for any OnSong users).  See you there!

Title	Artist	Key	Capo
Blue Suede Shoes	Carl Perkins - 1955	G		
Hard Days Night	John Lennon, Paul McCartney	G		
Hotel California	Don Felder, Don Henley, Glenn Frey	Em		
Walkin' After Midnight	Alan Block and Donn Hecht - 1957	C		
I'm a Believer	The Monkees	D

Lazy John	Traditional	F

St. James Infirmary Blues	Traditional	Em
