Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday, January 21 - Hancock Center

We'll be doing Swinging on a Star and Instanbul for the open mic on Feb 22. Attached is a PDF with those 2 songs - we may change some stuff as we work the arrangements - but this is the starting point. 

Attached is the weekly song PDF file (along with a .archive file for OnSong users). 

If you have a song you'd like the group to play - send it to tug_info at tuesdayukes.org   or ukemail at tuesdayukes.org   or just bring a copy to the meeting. We'll work it into the rotation.
