Here's what's up for the next TUG meeting Tuesday, January 30 - Hancock Center

The Open Mic is Saturday, Feb 22 at Kick Butt Coffee - They like to have the larger groups go first - so we'll be one of the first groups to play. We'll do a dress rehearsal at the Feb 18 meeting - with paper and music stands. We'll record the songs at the next meeting and I'll send those recordings out to everyone.  Attached are a PDF and .archive file with the songs for the open mic.


Attached is the weekly song PDF file (along with a .archive file for OnSong users). 

If you have a song you'd like the group to play - send it to tug_info at tuesdayukes.org   or ukemail at tuesdayukes.org   or just bring a copy to the meeting. We'll work it into the rotation.
