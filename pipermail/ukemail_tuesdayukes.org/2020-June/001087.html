I never noticed those numbers at the bottom of the chord shapes before. Is
that new?

Yes, I bar those strings with my ring finger. For that to work, you have to
bend your finger backwards enough to not touch the A string. I've also seen
it done with middle, ring, pinky on the fourth fret of G, C, E strings, and
index finger on fret 2 of A. I find that a little slow to get into
position, but that's only from lack of practice, I'm sure.

There is also the 1402 version of the E chord, but that seems harder than
either fingering of the 4442 E, or Walter's 4447.
