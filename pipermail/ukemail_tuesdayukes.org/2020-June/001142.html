Friends of Kevin (please forward):





Kevin's obituary should appear in Austin, San Antonio, and Bryan papers by this Thursday.  A proper celebration of his life will be held in Austin when conditions allow us to gather, but that may take months. In the meantime you can share memories of our remarkable friend and learn more about his life in several ways.  As anticipated last week, there will be a Zoom Wake this Thursday. A Memorial Website will be constructed to which you can contribute content.  You can also contribute images to a shared Photo Album. Details below.


  
 
  
 
The website,KJJ Memor​ial: Remembering the Life and Times of Kevin Jolly can be found here:https://www.kevinjolly.net/

  
 
Friends and family are welcome to submit stories (one story or theme at a time) with up to 5 images by sending an email tocraniumslows.kevinjolly at blogger.com. The subject line you use will be the title of your submission.  Content sent to this email address will be published on the memorial website ASAP. Kevin was active online and active in many areas. If you have links to anything Kevin-related please send them along to Marcus Wilson [mnw at member.fsf.org] who will update the list of links on the website.

  
 
  
 
The photo albumMemories of Kevin Jolly is found at: https://photos.app.goo.gl/VTA8mcTjgKgyEKgF7

  
 
If you have several images and want to contribute to the memorial photo album you are encouraged to use this form to share them:  https://forms.gle/wT7Epp6MdqXgAn6S8

All of the images will be collected and posted in the shared photo album. If the form won't work for you, please send images on to the blog email address above for sharing. 

  
 
  
 
Zoom Wake Remembering Kevin Jolly Thursday, June 25, 2020 04:30 PM Central Time

  
 
Keep in mind that there may be quite a few participants and time will be constrained, so please plan a story or memory you would like to share and by ready when your turn comes. Thanks.
