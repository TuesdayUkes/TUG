Hey Ukesters

We're trying to find ways to uke together - while we're all stuck in our separate houses.

Tomorrow night at 7:00 we're going to try an Online Open Mic using Zoom. You can use your desktop computer, tablet, or phone. You will need a microphone of some kind to play - but if you just want to hide and watch you just need a screen. Here's all you need to do:

1) Go sign up for Zoom at https://zoom.us/. It's free - and you can sign in using Google or Facebook if you have accounts in either of those places. Here's a link to their help for signing up if you have any problems: https://support.zoom.us/hc/en-us/articles/201362033-Getting-Started-on-Windows-and-Mac

2) Tuesday evening join the meeting at: https://zoom.us/j/942079843 The meeting ID is 942 079 843 in case you need it for something. 

3) There will be no sign up for the open mic - we'll just go around the group and folks who want to play can play. Let's plan on 1 song each to start, and then we'll go around again if we have time. 

We'll go for an hour - and after our regular meeting - as usual - some of us will meet for a beer and more ukulele playing at the TUG Beer Session  - https://zoom.us/j/777509092 - make sure you have your beer ready. 

If this works out we'll try to do this weekly. 

Hope to see you there!

Kevi
