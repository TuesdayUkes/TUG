Here's a little strumming sheet for what I call the Train Chunk Strum. This is a great strum for train songs - but also for a lot of blues tunes, and some rock n roll tunes as well. 

It incorporates the "Chunk" - which also fits in to a bunch of other strums.

There is also a video - here - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WcrN7PbvtW8

And also on the TUG web site - http://tuesdayukes.org/index.php/tug-strum-class/

Keep strumming!
