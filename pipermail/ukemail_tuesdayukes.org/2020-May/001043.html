We've figured out how to merge the song sheets with folk's videos to make new play along videos - 

In this new format you'll see a title screen, then the song sheet -so you can check out the chords - then the video will start with the lyrics scrolling. 

Grab your uke and play along to these 8 great videos from Paul and Walter - https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL-cb3zU_4CW4b1W5bCrHqZaQOJ5InwfVt

For anyone interested in doing a play along video, it's pretty simple

1) Pick a song that we have a song sheet for (attached is the Spring 2020 Songbook - but you could use any song we've done as a group)
2) Record yourself playing the tune and save the video as a .mov or .mp4 file. If you use a phone or tablet - record in landscape mode.
3) Upload the video to: https://www.dropbox.com/request/G6svgutQzAWJkHO9GXK0

We'll put it together with the song sheet and post it to You Tube to share with everyone. 

Thanks - and keep strumming!
