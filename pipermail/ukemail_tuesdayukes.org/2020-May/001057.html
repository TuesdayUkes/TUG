I spent a little time thinking about what the chords are. It looks like
I've only got 6 chords excluding the instrumental part (and that's only two
chord shapes), so the chords aren't too terrible -- the rhythm and
coordination between the left and right hands and voice are the challenge.
One thing I've had to work on is to get a steady strum -- I tend to rush
through some of the patterns. It seems to move so fast when you first start
learning it, but after a while -- on those occasions when things are
flowing well, it feels surprising slow.
