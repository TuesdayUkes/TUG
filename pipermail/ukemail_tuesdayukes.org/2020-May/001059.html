If you need a vacation - pick up your uke and play a few tunes - you won't think about being stuck at home, or work, or cleaning the bathroom - it's a quick, free getaway!

And since it's Two or Three Thursday - here's 3 great songs  2 or 3 chord songs to take you away -  Bye Bye Love, a great English folks tune - I'll Tell My Ma, and the one chord classic - Wang Dang Doodle. 

The song sheet is attached - and here's the you tube link - https://youtu.be/SAouYHkk-FA

And, as always, all of these (and more) are on the TUG web site - http://tuesdayukes.org/index.php/tug-member-videos/

Go play!
