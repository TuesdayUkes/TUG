﻿This Saturday, Nov. 14th, 2:00-3:30 we are planning an informal gathering
of interested ukulele players at Brushy Creek Regional Park.  This park was
mentioned at the last zoom meeting and several folks thought it was a
favorable location so we will give it a try.  It's located on Brushy Creek
Rd. off of Parmer.  I plan on arriving a bit earlier, around 1:30 to snag
an area for us.  If you have trouble locating the picnic area I've
selected, call my cell 512-739-3383.  Maybe one of the fellows can

thought.

chair just in case one isn't available.  Bring music stands if you have
them and something to secure your music if it's breezy.

keep social distance.

book. Here's one from April 2019:
