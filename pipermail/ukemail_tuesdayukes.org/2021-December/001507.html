Dear friends, I hope this message finds you happy, healthy and enjoying
some holiday time. *I'm writing to invite you to my annual "Twelve Chords
of Christmas" workshop tomorrow, December 28th at noon PST (3pm Eastern).*

This is a free online session (on Zoom) open to all. Given that it's the
season for jazz, we'll be exploring the rich, tasty sound of jazz chords
using the carol "The Twelve Days of Christmas." It's a casual, relaxed
workshop so don't worry if you don't have much experience with jazz. And,
better still, it's open to all tunings! *Handouts will be provided in
standard C6 tuning as well as D6 tuning and Baritone tuning!*

So, brush the crumbs out of your beard 🎅 and join me for some ukulele fun
tomorrow! Capacity is limited to 100 so please register now to reserve your
spot
