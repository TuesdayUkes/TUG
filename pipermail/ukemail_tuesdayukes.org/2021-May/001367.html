 Tomorrow we will have no Zoom! Since the weather looked maybe stormy last
week, we pushed our in-person meeting back a week to tomorrow. It still
looks rainy tomorrow, but the risk of serious storms looks lower than last
week, so cross your fingers.

Date:

Tuesday May 25, 2021, 6 PM

Location:

Oskar Blues (10420 Metric Blvd Ste 150)

Music:

We’ll be practicing music for Kevin's memorial (see attachments)

Comments:
1. We believe most folks will be vaccinated, but we can't guarantee it.
2. We encourage masks but it will be your decision once seated.
3. You might want to consider bringing a music stand.
4. Picnic tables with bench seats are on-site but you might consider
bringing a lawn chair to enable circular seating with greater distances.
