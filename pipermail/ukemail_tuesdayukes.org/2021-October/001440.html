"Since you went away
The days grow long
And soon I'll hear
Old winter's song”

We love Neil Degrasse Tyson.  We have even seen him in person.  My take away from what he said is that starting after June 21st, the longest day of the year, the days start getting shorter.  That would continue until December 21st, the shortest day of the year, and winter officially begins.  After that point the days start getting longer.  

So I think the lyrics have nothing to do with the actual length of the day and everything to do with the guy's broken heart.  He is lonely and bored, so to him the days do seem long.  And since he is observing the autumn leaves falling and faced with the approach of winter, he needs to call his girl friend and apologize to her for whatever bone headed thing he did.  If not, he’s going to need to find a therapist.
